ABOUT US

Welcome to KitDB, the world's most comprehensive football kit archive. Our mission is to catalog and celebrate the rich history and design of football shirts from every era, league, and club worldwide.

Our Story

Born out of a passion for the beautiful game and an obsession with its aesthetic evolution, KitDB was created to provide a centralized database for collectors, fans, and historians. Whether you are searching for that classic 90s geometric pattern or tracking the subtle changes in modern minimalist designs, we aim to document it all.

The Community

KitDB is built by and for the community. We encourage users to contribute, rate their favorite kits, and share their unique collections. Football is nothing without its fans, and the history of football kits is a shared cultural heritage we strive to preserve together.
